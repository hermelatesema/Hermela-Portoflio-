⬡ Capstone Project · UX Design

Digital ID Privacy Walkthrough

Role: UX Designer

Goal: Designed an interactive privacy education experience that helps users understand how everyday online behaviors such as scrolling, clicks, and time spent generate tracking signals and personalized content.

Process: Conducted user research through surveys to identify gaps in privacy understanding. Created user flows, wireframes, and interactive prototypes in Figma, then iterated on the experience based on usability feedback to improve clarity, accessibility, and engagement.

Takeaways: Learned that users understand privacy concerns better when tracking is visualized in a simple, interactive way. Guided experiences and clear UX patterns improved user comprehension and engagement.

Next Steps: Future development will focus on building the frontend and backend infrastructure, implementing real-time tracking visualization, and expanding the educational reporting system.
